Relax with family and friends in the middle of rural Andalusia.

A well as being the perfect venue for large family holidays it makes for a great venue for weddings, retreats and workshops. Almazara de Solerche is a beautifully renovated and decorated olive mill surrounded by the olive groves of the province of Cordoba.

Just 6km from Iznajar and just an hours drive from Cordoba, Malaga and Granada.

The cortijo is it is an ideal base from which to explore Andalucia, or to spend a week just relaxing by the pool. The cities of Malaga, Cordoba and Granda are all just 1 hours drive away, Seville is 1 hour 50 away and Marbella 1 and a half hours away.

Almazara de Solerche is the perfect holiday rental for large multi-generational groups and families. The accommodation sleeping up to 24 (when using additional sofa beds) is split over a main house and 3 apartments with 9 spacious and beautifully decorated bedrooms, all with en suite shower or bathrooms. The house and each apartment has its own kitchen and dining areas, making the layout ideal for groups made up of 3 or 4 families. The main house has 2 bedrooms and 2 lounges, one on the first floor which may be converted to a 10th bedroom. The kitchen is very large and leads to both the patio and hall, so is perfect for communal cooking and dining.



The Garden apartment is on the ground floor and has a kitchen/dining area and 2 suites (lounge and bedroom areas), 1 of which is wheelchair accessible, and plenty of space for adding an additional bed. The Patio apartment is on the first floor, has a kitchen/dining area, 2 twin rooms and a suite as well as terraces overlooking the front and back gardens. The White apartment has a twin room, a double room and a large open plan lounge/dining/kitchen area. As well as sharing the terraces with the patio apartment it has a further private terrace to the back.



There is a large hall measuring 140m2 with a huge fireplace, lounge and games area (pool table, table tennis and table football) which is ideal for dining, parties and celebrations. There is also another 70m2 lounge overlooking the pool which has WiFi internet and works well as a yoga studio and there are 2 patios to the rear of the cortijo which are perfect for dining outdoors.



The whole cortijo, its gardens and patios are surrounded by an old stone wall and the pool is completely fenced making the cortijo a great and safe place for children.



We can help with airport transits, chefs, musicians (from jazz to Flamenco), massage and yoga therapists to visit the cortijo.

Our Nearest Villages

Just 6km from both Iznajar and Rute the two villages have everything you need from supermarkets and banks to local restaurants and bars. Iznajar has an open air bar throughout August (Teatro de aire libre) where the ayuntamiento host film and music nights, as well as the spectacle of Semana Santa at Easter and the Feria at the start of September.
